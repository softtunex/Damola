Rubies UK
Website Design

Championing Seamless Transactions for UK Residents: Addressing the Unique Challenges of Immigrants.

Overview

Many UK residents are faced with challenges when sending money to loved ones, although many platforms appear to have come up with some solutions, Rubies UK goes beyond conventional offerings. This innovative platform not only facilitates seamless money transfers but also introduces a transformative concept—individualized debit cards for diverse financial purposes. Rubies UK stands as a beacon of financial inclusivity.

What the client asked

  • A clean website that displays all the features
  • Well-mapped-out navigation
  • Monetization

Solution

  • An intuitive, clean, and well-architected website was a solution to a great UX
  • Highlighting the USPs was also a key factor in meeting the business goals

My Role

I was responsible for conducting the UX research, designing wireframes, prototypes, usability testing, and making iterations based on the data collected and synthesizing user goals with business goals.

Approach

I had a brief session with the client to understand the business goals, then I used the design thinking process to uncover the user needs while accounting for business goals.

Tools Used

Empathize

Problem Definition
From the research, I was able to define the problem

  • HMW structure the page to enable users to access most important information easily?
  • HMW help users in making decision by selecting from the pricing plans available?
