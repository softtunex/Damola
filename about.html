Qui est-ce Adedamola ?

Hello there! I'm Adedamola Oyewale, a dynamic Product Designer weaving creativity into diverse industries. My academic roots in French and Education from the University of Benin.

 

At the early stage of my career, I worked as a Strategic Intelligence Analyst where I honed my skills in research, pattern recognition, clear communication and using data to shape informed decisions. Delving into Product Design, my passion lies in problem-solving across industries. I've left my creative imprint in fintech, traditional banking, adtech, agritech, edtech, gaming, monitoring & evaluation, and healthtech.

 

Passionate about helping businesses grow, I've contributed my design and problem-solving skills to 20+ projects enhancing their journeys and creating meaningful impacts. My unique approach involves infusing spontaneity into every project, igniting excitement. Completing each endeavor not only marks a milestone but triggers a surge of dopamine, a tangible reward for dedication.

 

Away from design, I find joy in horror movies and relish exploring new horizons. I'm also passionate about leadership programs that fuel my growth.

 

My story is about creating experiences that resonate, where every design tells a story and evokes emotions.
